Videogames

This section contains all things games.

This section is also under construction, but writings will appear on here.. eventually. While this is under construction, have an assorted, unsorted list of games I love.

  • Master of Orion 1 (1993) - One of the best turn based strategy games, ever released.
  • Marble Blast Gold (2003) - A platformer I've played on and off for 10+ years. Simple but infinitely replayable.
  • FTL: Faster Than Light (2012) - An excellent and a "tough-but-fair" indie roguelike, set in space!
  • Dungeon Keeper 1 (1997) - "Its good to be bad".
  • Europa Universalis IV (2013) - An great grand strategy game spanning late Medieval to early Modern ages. There's a reason I've sunk 1000+ hrs into this game.
